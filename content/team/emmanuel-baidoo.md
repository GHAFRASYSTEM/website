Emmanuel Baidoo, an agronomist, serves as the Treasurer of GHAFRA Nord. He is responsible for managing the association's finances, including dues collection, record keeping, and providing transparent financial reports to ensure accountability and trust within the organization. Passionate about community development, he works to support initiatives that empower and unite the Ghanaian community in Northern France. Motivated by his personal experiences navigating integration challenges in France, Emmanuel joined GHAFRA Nord to help build a strong support system that enables fellow Ghanaians to overcome barriers and access better opportunities. He began his role in February 2025.
