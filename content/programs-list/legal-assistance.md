GHA-FRA Nord assists members with visa renewal, residence permits, and legal issues, ensuring their rights are protected and upheld.

## What We Offer

- **Visa & Residence Permit Guidance**: Providing information and guidance on visa renewal processes and residence permit applications
- **Administrative Navigation**: Helping members understand and navigate French administrative procedures and bureaucracy
- **Legal Issue Support**: Connecting members with appropriate legal resources and support when facing legal challenges
- **Document Assistance**: Supporting members with translation needs and document preparation for official procedures

## How It Works

Members facing administrative or legal challenges can reach out to the association through our regular meetings or contact channels. Our network of experienced community members and partners provides guidance and, where possible, direct assistance.
