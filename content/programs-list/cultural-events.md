GHA-FRA Nord organizes a vibrant calendar of events and activities throughout the year, bringing the community together to celebrate Ghanaian culture and strengthen bonds.

## Annual Events Calendar

- **General Assembly** (January): The annual General Assembly is held every January, usually on the last Saturday of the month, to review the year and plan ahead
- **Freshers Welcome Party** (September): A warm welcome event for newly arrived Ghanaian students and community members at the start of the academic year
- **GHA-FRA Month** (October): October is officially GHA-FRA month, with all activities centered around highlighting and celebrating the association
- **GHA-FRA Bronya Afahy3** (December): The annual Christmas celebration bringing the entire community together for festive cheer

## Regular Activities

- **Monthly Meetings**: Regular meetings to discuss issues, developments, and planning within GHA-FRA
- **Saturday Sports**: Weekly sporting activities held at Complexe Sportif Jacqueline Auriol (Wazemmes) every Saturday
- **Courtesy Calls**: Official visits to the Head of Consular at the Embassy of Ghana in Paris
